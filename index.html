Common questions from GCs and owners

Direct answers — no qualifier clutter.

Ask us directly →
  • What areas does GCC LLC serve?

    Kansas City (KCMO) and St. Louis (STL) metros. KCMO coverage includes inside the 435 ring plus Johnson and Wyandotte counties. STL covers Greater St. Louis, Metro East (Illinois), and St. Charles County. We also bid in Missouri, Kansas, and Illinois statewide (Illinois case-by-case).

  • How quickly can you return a bid?

    48-hour SLA. Send a plan set and we return a complete package inside two business days. We bid as drawn — no outbound calls to the GC during the bid window. If a spec is ambiguous we document the assumption, exclusion, or density allowance in §3–§4 of the proposal.

  • What warranty do you provide?

    Lifetime workmanship and parts on all GCC-installed components. The warranty transfers with the property — the next tenant or owner inherits it at no cost. Cabling manufacturer system warranties (15–25 years) are passed through separately when we install under a certified-installer program.

  • Do you carry GL insurance and workers’ comp?

    Yes. Next Insurance GL ($1M per occurrence / $2M aggregate) and AmGUARD WC (NAIC #42390, statutory). ACORD certificates and W9 same day on request. Bonding capacity letters available for projects requiring bid, performance, or payment bonds.

  • What’s the difference between Division 27 and Division 28?

    Division 27 (Communications) covers structured cabling — Cat6A copper, fiber, telecom rooms, WAP runs, and DAS pathways. Division 28 (Electronic Safety & Security) covers access control, IP cameras, intrusion detection, and intercom. GCC self-performs both on commercial, institutional, and residential projects.

  • Do you handle prevailing wage and certified payroll?

    Yes. GCC handles prevailing wage and certified payroll on public jobs. Compliance docs — CPL, prevailing wage cert, EMR letter, and site safety plan — furnished on request. Open-shop; we work private and public alike.

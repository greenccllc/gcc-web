Services

Everything we run, terminate, mount, drill, and document.

If it carries low-voltage signal, we probably touch it. Below is the actual service catalog — what's included, how it gets tested, and the standards we hold ourselves to. No upsell brochure-speak.

Division 27

Telecommunications & Structured Cabling

The bones of every modern building. We pull, terminate, certify, label, and document — then hand you an as-built that future-you (or the next IT guy) will actually read.

Horizontal Cabling

Cat6 and Cat6A copper drops to every workstation, AP, camera, and door. Plenum-rated jackets, velcro management, J-hooks where they belong.

  • Cat6 / Cat6A · plenum / riser
  • Coax (RG6 / RG11) where needed
  • Velcro only — no zip-ties on data bundles
  • Brady label printer (TIA-606-B compliant)

Fiber Backbone

Single-mode and multimode runs, riser between IDFs, OS2 between buildings. Fusion-spliced where it counts, mechanical connectors where it makes sense.

  • OM3 / OM4 multimode
  • OS1 / OS2 single-mode
  • LC, SC, MTP/MPO terminations
  • OTDR-tested with full trace report
️

Telecom Room Build-Out

Server racks, patch panels, ladder rack, cable management arms, grounding to TIA-607. The rack you'd be embarrassed not to photograph at closeout.

  • Open-frame & cabinet racks
  • Patch panels, copper & fiber
  • TIA-607 grounding + bonding
  • Closeout photos with every project

Wireless Infrastructure

Wi-Fi 6 and Wi-Fi 7 site survey, AP placement, mounting, and cabling. Coverage you can stand under with full bars, not "kinda OK if you sit by the window."

  • Predictive heat-map design
  • UniFi U7 Pro / Enterprise placement
  • Mesh planning for tough spaces
  • Post-install validation walk

Testing & Certification

Fluke DSX-8000 for copper, OTDR for fiber. Every drop tested, every result archived, all as-builts handed over electronically.

  • Fluke DSX-8000 channel/permanent link
  • OTDR fiber traces (multimode + single-mode)
  • PDF cert reports per drop
  • Pass / fail summary spreadsheet

Design & As-Builts

Plan analysis from T-sheets, E-sheets, and A600s. Scope drawings before install, electronic as-builts after. Updates to the closeout package as long as you own the building.

  • LV plan analysis from contract docs
  • Pre-install scope drawings
  • Bluebeam-marked as-builts
  • Editable source files at closeout
Division 28

Electronic Safety & Security

Cameras, doors, intercoms, and emergency communication — installed by the same crew that ran the cable, so nothing falls through the trade gap.

📹

IP Surveillance (CCTV)

Ubiquiti G6 cameras with AI analytics, NVR storage, and a UI your staff will actually use when something happens at 3am.

  • Ubiquiti G6 / G6 Pro / Bullet
  • Network video recorder + retention plan
  • AI motion / person / vehicle detection
  • Mobile app remote viewing
🚪

Access Control

Door hubs, magnetic locks, card readers, intercoms. Schedules, holidays, and audit logs that pass an HR review.

  • UniFi Access door hubs
  • Card, fob, NFC, mobile credentials
  • Schedules, exceptions, lockdown
  • Full audit log + alerts
🆘

Area of Rescue

Code-required two-way emergency communication for stairwells and refuge areas. UL-listed equipment, full closeout package for the AHJ.

  • Stair shaft & refuge area stations
  • Master annunciator at fire control
  • UL 2017 listed equipment
  • Closeout package for AHJ sign-off
🔔

Intercom & Paging

Doorbell-cam intercoms at entrances, overhead paging that doesn't sound like a 1987 drive-thru, and integration with phone systems.

  • IP intercom (UniFi, 2N, Doorbird)
  • Overhead paging (Algo, CyberData)
  • SIP integration with phone system
  • Schedule-based zone paging

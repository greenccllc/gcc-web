Adding new staff or admins

For security, accounts must be self-created. Have the new teammate register at greencommllc.com/clients/ using their work email — they'll start as a client. Then come back here and switch their role to staff or admin from the dropdown.

All role changes are logged in the activity feed and take effect on the user's next page load.

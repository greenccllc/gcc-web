Intuit QuickBooks Online integration

Sync your QuickBooks data with GCC LLC

Connect your QuickBooks Online account to mirror invoices, payments, and customer records into your GCC LLC project portal. Automatic — no manual data entry, no exports, no spreadsheets.

Sign in to connect → Talk to us first

What syncs

  • Customer records (name, address, billing email)
  • Invoices (status, line items, balances)
  • Payments and credits
  • Items and service catalog
  • Real-time updates via Intuit webhooks

How it works

  1. Sign in to your GCC LLC portal as an administrator
  2. Click "Connect QuickBooks" — you'll be redirected to Intuit
  3. Authorize GCC LLC to read your QuickBooks data
  4. Intuit redirects you back; we store an encrypted refresh token
  5. Initial sync pulls the latest 50 invoices; webhooks keep them current
OAuth scope requested: com.intuit.quickbooks.accounting (read access to accounting data only — we do not request payments scope)

Security & data handling

OAuth refresh tokens are encrypted at rest using ASP.NET Data Protection with machine-bound keys. We never store your QuickBooks password. You can disconnect at any time from your portal Settings page or from inside QuickBooks under Apps → My Apps → Disconnect.

Disconnect

To revoke this integration, sign in to your portal and visit Financials → Disconnect, or open QuickBooks Online and remove GCC LLC from the connected apps list. Your historical sync data remains in your portal; only the live connection ends.

Scope at a glance

Cat6A plenum, every drop

Baseline included silently. Cat6 substitute is never offered. The 25-year backbone life is paid for once and inherited by the next tenant.

Fiber backbone

OM3/OM4 multimode for in-building backbones. OS1/OS2 single-mode for inter-building, campus, or carrier handoff. OTDR trace per strand.

Telecom rooms (TIA-607 / 569)

Bonded ground bar, ladder rack, vertical + horizontal cable management, patch panel labeling, UPS sizing, environmental monitoring optional.

Certification + as-builts

Fluke DSX-8000 channel + permanent link results. Bluebeam-marked drawing set. Editable source files at handover.

Wi-Fi design + survey

Predictive + post-installation site surveys. AP placement walked against the actual walls, not the floor plan. Wi-Fi 6 / 6E / 7 mounts and trim included.

Demolition + clean-up

Abandoned-cable removal per NEC 800 / 770. We don't leave the riser stuffed with the last guy's spaghetti.

Standards we hold to

BICSI / TIA-568

Cabling performance, channel and permanent-link models, application support.

TIA-606-B

Labeling and identifier scheme. Both ends of every cable, both ends of every patch cord, plus the administration record.

TIA-607

Grounding and bonding for telecom rooms. Bonded TGB, ladder bonded to TGB, equipment bonded back.

NEC / UL

Plenum jackets in air-handling spaces. UL-listed components only. Listed firestop where the cable crosses a rated assembly.

Brady labels

Heat-shrink for fiber, vinyl wrap for copper. Printed on-site, never hand-written.

Fluke DSX-8000

Channel + permanent link. LinkWare PDFs per drop. Re-test on warranty claims, no charge.

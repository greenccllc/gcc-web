Common questions

Cat6 or Cat6A?

Cat6A plenum — included silently on every commercial drop. We don't offer Cat6 substitutes. The 25-year backbone life is paid once and inherited by the next tenant.

Do you certify every run?

Yes. Fluke DSX-8000 channel + permanent-link results, LinkWare PDFs per drop, summary spreadsheet at closeout. Re-test on warranty claims at no charge.

Telecom room build-out?

Yes — bonded ground bar to TIA-607, ladder rack, cable management, patch-panel labeled per TIA-606-B. Brady labels printed on-site, never hand-written.

What fiber types?

OM3/OM4 multimode for in-building backbones; OS1/OS2 single-mode for inter-building, campus, or carrier handoffs. OTDR trace per strand in the closeout package.

Abandoned cable removal?

Yes. Per NEC 800/770. We pull old cable out of the riser before the new cable goes in, documented in the as-built with item count, floor, and disposition.

How far do you travel?

KCMO + STL metros as primary territory. We bid across Missouri, Kansas, and Illinois. Outside the metros, mobilization is itemized in §3 of the proposal.

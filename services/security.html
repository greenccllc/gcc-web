What we install

Ubiquiti G6 cameras

4K and 8 MP variants, IR or night-color, fixed and PTZ. POE-only — no DC injectors hidden behind drywall.

UniFi Access doors

Door hubs, mag locks or strikes, REX + door contacts, mobile credential and NFC card. Lockdown mode on a single button press.

IP intercom + paging

Algo, CyberData, 2N, Doorbird front-door units. Overhead paging zoned by floor or department. SIP-routed where it makes sense.

NVR storage + retention

30/60/90-day retention sized to your camera count + bitrate. Network or local NVR — the math goes in §3 of the proposal so you can audit it.

Analytics

Person, vehicle, animal, package, license-plate detection running on the recorder, not the cloud. Smart Detection alerts wired to email or push.

Cabling colors that match the spec

Green for camera, red for access. Different cable colors per system mean a future tech can find the run without a tone-and-probe afternoon.

What we don’t bid

Listing the exclusions up front saves a re-scope after award.

Fire alarm

Out of scope. We coordinate with the FA contractor, but we don’t bid head-end equipment, devices, or AHJ closeout.

Area-of-rescue (AoR)

Out of scope. UL 2017 / IBC 1009 systems are coordinated like fire alarm — the AoR contractor owns head-end and station install, AHJ closeout, and integration testing.

Mass notification beyond standard PA

If the spec calls for ECS / EVAC integrated with FA, that lives with the FA scope.

What lifetime warranty actually covers

Workmanship — lifetime

Anything we cabled, terminated, mounted, or programmed. If a connector fails because of how we crimped it, we re-run the drop.

Parts — lifetime, GCC-installed only

Components we sold and installed are warrantied for the life of the property. Customer-owned gear is warrantied per its manufacturer terms.

Transfers with the property

Closeout documentation hands over with the deed. The next owner gets the same warranty terms — no re-registration call.

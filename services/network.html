Common questions

What hardware do you install?

UniFi end-to-end — Dream Machine Pro for routing, UniFi switches for POE, U7 Pro APs for Wi-Fi. One controller, one firmware track, one place to manage everything.

Do you set up VLANs?

Yes. Corporate, guest, IoT, voice, security camera, and BYOD each get their own VLAN — documented in the as-built. No undocumented flat networks.

What's in Monthly Care?

24/7 controller monitoring, firmware on a tested release track, config backups, quarterly health report, and an after-hours dispatch budget.

Can you support existing Cisco / Meraki gear?

Yes, case-by-case. We prefer UniFi for integration, but we'll support existing hardware and tell you straight if a replacement makes sense — or doesn't.

Wi-Fi 7 or 6E?

Depends on your density. We run a predictive survey and give you a straight answer — 7 helps at high client density, 6E is still right for most light-office or retail.

What size is the Managed Services tier?

Sized by drop count: Small (≤50), Team (≤200), Enterprise (≤500), Mega (500+). Included as an alternate line item on every whole-building commercial bid.

Curious about Wi-Fi 7?

We can run a predictive survey on your floor plan and tell you whether 7 buys you anything over 6E for your density. Twenty-minute call, no commitment.

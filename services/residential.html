What we wire

Whole-home Cat6 + fiber

One drop per TV, one per room, two per office. Plenum where the duct runs through it. Patch-panel terminated in a media closet, labeled per TIA-606-B even on residential.

Wi-Fi 7 mesh

Predictive design first, AP walk before mount. Coverage where you actually use the phone — not just where the access point looks symmetrical.

Cameras + doorbell

Ubiquiti G6 cameras, AI alerts on package + person, smart doorbell that doesn't subscribe you to a cloud you can't leave.

Smart home

Govee strip lighting, Alexa or Google Home, Ubiquiti integrated locks, smart shades. Scenes that survive a power cycle.

TV mounts + AV

Articulating mounts behind drywall power kits, surround setup, multi-room audio. Done so the wires don't sag.

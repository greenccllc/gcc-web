What we wire

Whole-home Cat6 + fiber

One drop per TV, one per room, two per office. Plenum where the duct runs through it. Patch-panel terminated in a media closet, labeled per TIA-606-B even on residential.

Wi-Fi 7 mesh

Predictive design first, AP walk before mount. Coverage where you actually use the phone — not just where the access point looks symmetrical.

Cameras + doorbell

Ubiquiti G6 cameras, AI alerts on package + person, smart doorbell that doesn't subscribe you to a cloud you can't leave.

Smart home

Govee strip lighting, Alexa or Google Home, Ubiquiti integrated locks, smart shades. Scenes that survive a power cycle.

TV mounts + AV

Articulating mounts behind drywall power kits, surround setup, multi-room audio. Done so the wires don't sag.

Common questions

Do you work in occupied homes?

Yes. We stage tools out of traffic areas, clean up each day, and schedule around your household — not around our convenience.

What smart home platforms?

Alexa, Google Home, Apple HomeKit, Ubiquiti UniFi, and Govee. We wire for the platform you already use — no ecosystem switch required.

Will you do just one room?

Yes. Single-room jobs, single-device installs, and single-problem fixes are all welcome. No minimum package required to book.

Do you do kitchen or bath renovations?

Yes — smart shower, heated floor, under-cabinet lighting, vent-hood replacement, fencing, decking, and the auxiliary trades alongside the LV scope.

TV mounting?

Yes — articulating mounts, behind-drywall power kits, in-wall HDMI or network pass-through, and surround-sound setup. Wires don't sag.

What does the lifetime warranty cover?

Workmanship + parts on GCC-installed components for the life of the property. Transfers to the next owner with no re-registration call.

About

A small, sharp Missouri LLC. Two owners. A reliable bench of subs. No corporate brochure.

We're built deliberately small. The CEO answers her own phone. The CTO terminated the cable. The work gets done by people whose names we know.

Story

Why we exist

Most low-voltage shops are either too small to be taken seriously on commercial work, or so big they treat your project as a number on a project manager's spreadsheet. We started GCC to sit in the middle: institutional-grade standards, owner-on-site delivery.

Kaitlyn (CEO) ran medical and administrative operations for years — the part of contracting where a missed compliance check, a sloppy COI, or a late invoice loses the job. Nathan (CTO) spent a decade in the field as a foreman pulling cable, building telecom rooms, and untangling other people's "we'll fix it later" decisions. We brought those two halves together in early 2026, picked up our first jobs, and kept going.

Today we serve the Kansas City and St. Louis metros from O'Fallon, MO. We're licensed, insured, bonded, and registered as a WBE/MBE — which matters for set-aside contracts in government, education, and healthcare. The certifications matter; the work matters more.

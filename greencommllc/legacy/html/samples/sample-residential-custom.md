## 04 — What We're Installing

**Home Network.** We'll install future-proof network cable from a central panel to twelve spots in your house — the two TVs, your office, the kitchen island, the smart-home hub location, and each of the four WiFi access points. The panel sits in the basement mechanical room near the existing electrical panel, and from there every cable routes to wherever you need it.

Equipment we'll install:

- Twelve data drops (the outlets you see in each room)
- Four ceiling-mounted WiFi access points for whole-home coverage (primary bedroom, basement gym, main level, upstairs hallway)
- One structured-media panel — 42-inch recessed, with hinged cover
- Router, switch, and AP controller (equipment listed below)

**Home Security.** Five cameras total: four exterior (front door, back patio, driveway, side yard) and one interior covering the mudroom / garage entry. All cameras are IP-based, PoE-powered from the network switch (no separate power runs), and record to a local network video recorder that stays in the panel.

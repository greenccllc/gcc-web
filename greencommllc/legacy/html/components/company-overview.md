# Component: Company Overview

**Use in:** GC proposals (Section 01 or 04) · Owner proposals (Section 01 "About GCC") · Residential (Section 01 "Who you're working with")

---

## Long form (Owner & GC proposals — ~150 words)

GCC LLC is a Division 27 / Division 28 low-voltage contractor serving the Kansas City and St. Louis metros. We furnish and install structured cabling, electronic security, audio-visual, and telecommunications room infrastructure for commercial, institutional, and residential clients.

Every data drop is Cat6A plenum, included in our baseline scope at no upcharge over Cat6 specifications. Every copper termination is certified with a Fluke DSX-8000 to TIA/EIA-568 Class EA, with LinkWare PDF reports delivered at closeout. Every fiber run is OTDR-characterized and LSPM-verified. Every label follows TIA-606-C Class 3 conventions, printed on a Brady M611.

GCC is licensed and insured for commercial general liability ($1M / $2M), workers' compensation (statutory), and employer's liability ($1M). Bid, performance, and payment bonds are available per project. Lifetime workmanship warranty transfers with the property.

# Component: Certifications & Standards

**Use in:** Every proposal. Under "How we deliver" or "Standards of work."

---

## Standards cited on every commercial proposal

- **TIA/EIA-568-D** — Commercial building telecommunications cabling standard
- **TIA-569-E** — Pathways and spaces for commercial buildings
- **TIA-606-C** — Administration standard for telecommunications infrastructure (labeling, Class 3 minimum)
- **TIA-607-C** — Generic telecommunications bonding and grounding (earthing)
- **TIA-862-B** — Intelligent-building systems cabling (where applicable)
- **NFPA 70 (NEC)** — National Electrical Code, Articles 725, 770, 800
- **BICSI TDMM** — Telecommunications Distribution Methods Manual
- **NEC 800.179** — Plenum-rated CMP cabling in air handling spaces
- **ADA** — Accessibility compliance for reach ranges and device mounting

## Testing & certification equipment (owned in-house, not rented)

- **Fluke DSX-8000 CableAnalyzer** — Cat5e / Cat6 / Cat6A permanent-link and channel certification to Class EA. LinkWare PDF per run delivered at closeout.
- **OTDR** — Optical time-domain reflectometer for single-mode and multi-mode fiber characterization.
- **LSPM** — Light source / power meter for end-to-end fiber loss measurement.
- **Brady M611** — TIA-606-C compliant label printer.

## Closeout deliverables (standard on every job)

- As-built drawings (PDF + CAD on request)
- LinkWare PDF certification report — every port, dated, signed
- OTDR trace files per fiber strand (where fiber in scope)
- Rack elevations and photo-free cable inventory
- Warranty registration and certificate
- Product data sheets and MSDS index

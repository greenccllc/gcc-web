# Component: Differentiators (the 4 bullets)

**Use in:** Every proposal. Primary location — Cover Letter + Section 01 Executive Summary. Secondary — footer of Bid Overview.

These four bullets are the canonical GCC LLC differentiators. They appear word-for-word — never paraphrased, never expanded, never reordered.

---

## The 4 bullets (gold-dotted · Forest Green text · Calibri 11pt)

- **Lifetime workmanship warranty** — transfers with the property
- **100% on-time delivery record**
- **Fluke DSX-8000 certification on every cable** — LinkWare PDF at closeout
- **Cat6A plenum included on every data drop** — no upcharge vs Cat6 spec

---

## Hero callout (Section 06 Warranty — opens with this)

**LIFETIME WORKMANSHIP — TRANSFERS WITH THE PROPERTY**

Every cable we install, every termination we make, for as long as the infrastructure is in service. Manufacturer warranties pass through on equipment. Five-year coverage on GCC-furnished hardware.

---

## Do not expand with

- Adjectives like "best," "leading," "premier"
- Additional bullets or sub-bullets
- Rewording to match client jargon

## Gold-dot styling

Before each of the four bullets, render a Warm Gold (`#D4AF37`) filled circle (●) followed by a space. Gold usage is capped at 2× per bid doc or 3× per quote — these four bullets count as a single usage.

# Standard Terms & Conditions

**Included verbatim in the T&C section of every proposal.** Residential proposals use a shorter version (marked below).

Reviewed by legal counsel. Modifications require Kaitlyn's signoff.

---

## 1. Acceptance and Validity

This proposal is valid for sixty (60) days from the date of issue. Client acceptance is evidenced by a signed contract, signed purchase order, written acceptance of this proposal, or issuance of a notice-to-proceed. Once accepted, this document and any attached scope descriptions become part of the contract documents and prevail over conflicting prior discussions, subject to the order of precedence in the master contract.

## 2. Scope Interpretation

Work is limited to the items described in this proposal's Scope of Work section and any exhibits attached by reference. Items not explicitly included are excluded, including but not limited to the items in Section 9 ("Exclusions") of this proposal. Work not described cannot be assumed from industry practice, drawings not referenced in this proposal, or verbal discussions.

## 3. Changes in Scope (Change Orders)

Any change to the scope, specification, quantity, schedule, or design requires a written change order executed by both parties. Change orders are priced on the same rate card as the base scope, plus a ten percent (10%) coordination markup. GCC will not begin any change-order work until the change order is signed. Verbal directives do not authorize change-order work.

## 4. Schedule

Schedule dates are contingent on timely award, receipt of specified materials, access to the work area, and readiness of preceding trades. Delays caused by others entitle GCC to an equivalent extension of time. Consequential-damages claims are waived by both parties.

## 5. Price and Payment

### Commercial

Progress billing occurs monthly based on percent-complete, submitted by the 25th of the month for work performed through that date. Retention is five percent (5%) unless otherwise specified in the master contract, released upon substantial completion and punch-list clearance. Payment terms are Net 30 from invoice date. Late payments accrue 1.5% per month (18% annual) or the maximum legal rate, whichever is lower.

### Residential

Thirty percent (30%) deposit is due at contract signing. Forty percent (40%) is due on material delivery to site. Thirty percent (30%) is due on substantial completion and homeowner walkthrough. Payment by check or ACH is free; credit-card payments via Stripe are subject to a 3% surcharge.

## 6. Lien Rights

GCC reserves its rights under Missouri mechanic's lien law (RSMo 429). Partial lien waivers are provided with each progress payment on request. Final lien waivers are provided with final payment.

## 7. Warranty

**Lifetime workmanship warranty** on all labor performed by GCC, transferring with the property to subsequent owners. Every cable installed, every termination made, for as long as the infrastructure is in service. **Five-year warranty** on GCC-furnished hardware. Manufacturer warranties on equipment pass through to the client. Warranty claims must be reported to GCC in writing within a reasonable period of discovery.

Warranty excludes: acts of God, misuse, unauthorized modifications, damage caused by other trades or subsequent work by parties other than GCC, and normal wear on user-replaceable parts (batteries, fuses, lamps).

## 8. Insurance and Indemnity

GCC maintains commercial general liability, workers' compensation, and employer's liability insurance as stated in the Insurance section of this proposal. Additional-insured endorsements and waivers of subrogation are provided at no cost upon request. Indemnification is mutual, each party holding the other harmless for its own negligent acts, to the extent permitted by Missouri law.

## 9. Exclusions

Unless explicitly included in this proposal's Scope of Work, the following are excluded:

- Fire alarm systems and FACP work (NICET scope)
- ERCES / BDA / DAS public-safety in-building radio
- Lighting controls (WaveLinx, nLight, DLM, Lutron)
- Building management systems and HVAC controls (Forge, Metasys, Niagara, Desigo)
- EV charger network data
- Mass notification beyond standard paging speakers
- Permit fees beyond low-voltage permits (electrical permits, building permits, AHJ fees)
- Temporary power, lighting, restrooms, dumpsters, hoists, and other GC-furnished site services
- Hazardous materials abatement
- Structural engineering
- Cutting, patching, or repair of walls, ceilings, or finishes beyond a 3"×3" area around each penetration
- Owner-furnished equipment programming (VoIP server, VMS software, access-control database)

## 10. Termination

Either party may terminate this agreement upon written notice for cause if the other party materially breaches and fails to cure within thirty (30) days of written notice. GCC is entitled to payment for all work performed and materials on order as of the termination date, plus demobilization labor.

## 11. Dispute Resolution

The parties will first attempt to resolve disputes through good-faith negotiation. Unresolved disputes will be submitted to non-binding mediation in O'Fallon, Missouri before litigation. This agreement is governed by the laws of the State of Missouri. Venue for any action is St. Charles County, Missouri.

## 12. Safety and Site Conditions

GCC crews are OSHA 10 trained at the installer level and OSHA 30 trained at the supervisor level. Two-person minimum on site at all times. GCC follows the site-safety plan of the general contractor or owner, in addition to GCC's own safety program.

## 13. Miscellaneous

This proposal, together with any executed contract and its attached exhibits, represents the complete agreement between the parties. It supersedes all prior discussions and proposals. Modifications require a writing signed by both parties. If any provision is held unenforceable, the remainder shall remain in force.

# Scope — Telecom Room Buildout (Commercial)

**Division:** 27 11 00 (Communications Equipment Room Fittings)
**Applies to:** New MDFs, IDFs, demarc rooms, and equipment closets.

---

## Inclusions

- 42U server-grade rack installation — bolted to floor, ground-bar-kit bonded, blanking panels on unused slots
- MDF / IDF layout per telecom-room drawings
- Rack dress-out — horizontal wire managers, vertical wire managers, D-rings
- Plywood backboards — 3/4" fire-rated, painted gray, ground-bar-kit mounted to backboard at 12" AFF
- Ground-bar kit — TMGB (Telecommunications Main Grounding Busbar) in MDF, TGB (Telecommunications Grounding Busbar) in each IDF, tied back to building grounding electrode system per NEC 250 and TIA-607-C
- Patch panels — 48-port 1U or 2U Cat6A shielded as specified, labeled per TIA-606-C Class 3
- Cable trays or ladder racks above the rack for overhead cable entry
- Environmental rough-in preparation — knockouts and plates for HVAC supply, if specified

## Deliverables

- Rack elevations — one per rack, photo-free diagram
- Grounding diagram — TMGB / TGB / rack bonding
- Label schedule
- Backboard layout drawing

## Exclusions

- 120V / 208V electrical circuits (by electrical contractor)
- HVAC sizing, supply and return ductwork, thermostatic controls (by HVAC contractor)
- UPS units, PDU selection beyond basic rack-mount strips
- Fire suppression (pre-action, clean-agent) — by fire protection contractor
- Backboard fire-treatment inspection / signoff — AHJ responsibility

## Standards

TIA-569-E · TIA-607-C · NEC Articles 250, 770, 800 · BICSI TDMM

# Scope — Structured Cabling (Commercial)

**Division:** 27 05 00 · 27 10 00 · 27 13 00 · 27 15 00
**Applies to:** All commercial bids where data, voice, or composite cabling is in scope.

---

## Inclusions

- Cat6A plenum (CMP) UTP cable, 4-pair 23AWG, from nearest IDF / MDF to each data outlet shown on the E-series drawings. **Cat6A plenum is included on every drop at no upcharge vs. Cat6 specification.**
- Composite cable runs for IP cameras, wireless access points, access-control devices, and paging speakers where shown.
- Copper terminations — T568B wiring, Cat6A keystone jacks at each outlet, patch panels at the IDF / MDF. 48-port rack-mount patch panels assumed unless otherwise specified.
- Fluke DSX-8000 permanent-link and channel certification to TIA/EIA-568 Class EA. Every port tested and documented. **LinkWare PDF delivered at closeout.**
- Single-mode or multi-mode fiber backbone where shown — pre-terminated or field-terminated with fusion or mechanical splicing. OTDR characterization and LSPM end-to-end loss testing on every strand.
- Coax RG6 sweep testing where TV drops are in scope.
- TIA-606-C Class 3 labeling on every outlet, every patch panel port, every rack, every cable at each end. Brady M611 printed labels (thermal transfer, TIA-606-C compliant).
- Cable management — horizontal wire managers, D-rings, velcro hook-and-loop at the rack.

## Deliverables

- LinkWare PDF certification report — every port, dated and signed
- OTDR trace files per fiber strand, .sor format
- As-built drawings — PDF + CAD on request
- Rack elevations and TIA-606-C label schedule
- Product data sheets and warranty registration

## Exclusions

- Conduit, raceway, and fire-stopping where not explicitly called out (see Pathway & Support scope)
- 120V or higher electrical work
- Backboards or plywood installation (see Telecom Room Buildout scope)
- Device-level programming for third-party systems (e.g., VoIP phone server, access-control head-end database)

## Standards

TIA/EIA-568-D · TIA-569-E · TIA-606-C · TIA-607-C · NEC Articles 725, 770, 800 · BICSI TDMM

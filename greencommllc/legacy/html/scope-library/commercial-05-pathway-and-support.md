# Scope — Pathway & Support (Commercial)

**Division:** 27 05 28 (Pathways for Communications Systems)
**Applies to:** Conduit, cable tray, J-hooks, firestop, and core drilling for low-voltage runs.

---

## Inclusions

- Cable tray — ladder-rack or wire-basket (Cablofil-style), horizontal runs above drop ceilings
- J-hook installation at 48" maximum spacing per TIA-569-E
- EMT conduit stubs from wall plates through finished ceilings to accessible space above
- Sleeves through rated walls and floors, sized per NEC 800.113
- Firestop — **3M** or **Hilti** UL-listed through-penetration assemblies, sleeve-fill putty or intumescent pillows per penetration
- Core drilling through non-structural masonry or slab (up to 4" diameter) where shown
- Fire-rated wall openings documented with annotated photographs for AHJ review (photos are for construction QA only, not included in client deliverables)

## Deliverables

- Firestop schedule — every penetration with UL assembly number and product SKU
- Pathway riser diagram (as-built)

## Exclusions

- Structural penetrations (requires structural engineer signoff)
- Concrete saw-cutting beyond 4" core
- Trenching for outside-plant fiber
- Roof penetrations (by roofing contractor)
- Fire-rated wall repair beyond firestop (by drywall contractor)

## Standards

TIA-569-E · NEC Article 800.113 · UL firestop assembly listings · NFPA 70

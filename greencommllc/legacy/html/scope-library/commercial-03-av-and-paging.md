# Scope — Audio-Visual & Paging (Commercial)

**Division:** 27 41 00 (Audio-Video Systems) · 27 51 19 (Public Address)
**Applies to:** Conference rooms, classrooms, common areas, retail, restaurant, and overhead paging.

---

## Inclusions — AV

- HDMI active extenders — HDBaseT over Cat6A for long-run scenarios (>50 ft), transmitter and receiver
- Conference-room rough-in — floor boxes, poke-through cores, wall plates with HDMI and Cat6A pass-through
- Display mounting — flat-panel wall mounts up to 86"; larger displays quoted separately
- Low-voltage wiring for projectors, including ceiling mount-plate rough-in
- Assistive-listening induction loops where specified

## Inclusions — Paging

- 70V overhead paging speaker installation — ceiling-tile or pendant mount
- Paging amplifier rack install and tap-off (amp is typically owner-furnished head-end equipment)
- Paging zoning per design drawings
- Integration with phone system paging relay (dry-contact close)

## Inclusions — TV Distribution

- RG6 coax TV drops from head-end to each TV location
- Coax sweep testing and documentation

## Deliverables

- Rack-and-stack elevations for AV rack
- Cable schedule and zone map for paging
- Closeout photo-free as-built (label schedule + one-line diagrams)

## Exclusions

- AV control-system programming (Crestron, Extron, AMX) — GCC installs the plates and wiring; control-system integrator programs the system
- Video-conferencing codec configuration (Zoom Rooms, Teams Rooms, Webex) — GCC installs wiring and mounts; IT commissions the codec
- Content-distribution encoders / decoders for large IPTV systems
- Audio DSP programming (Biamp, QSC) — GCC installs hardware; acoustician or integrator tunes

## Standards

InfoComm / AVIXA best practices · ADA assistive-listening compliance · NEC 725/800

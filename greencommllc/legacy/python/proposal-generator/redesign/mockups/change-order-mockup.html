Why this change order

Delta  ·  2 / 3
Reason

During the 2026-04-14 pre-construction walkdown with MOCSA facilities and the architect of record, four additional interior doors were identified as needing access-control coverage that were not in the original GCC-2023-019 scope: the IT closet (room 142), the records room (room 148), and two client-intake doors on the north corridor. This CO adds the rough-in, device labor, and head-end programming for those four doors only. All other executed scope under GCC-2023-019 remains in force without modification.

The added work is additive and non-disruptive. It runs in parallel with the existing scope and does not push the project finish date. The pricing below uses the same unit rates that were established in GCC-2023-019, with no mobilization surcharge because our crew is already on site.

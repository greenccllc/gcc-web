GCC LLC is pleased to submit our bid for the William Chrisman High School 2026 Renovations — Low-Voltage Package, in response to bid documents dated 2026-03-12, bid number ISD-2026-014. Pricing is held firm for 60 days from the issue date and complies with the General Contract terms, Division 27/28 specifications, and Missouri prevailing-wage requirements for public work.

Independence School District is renovating two academic wings and the competition gym at William Chrisman High School during the 2026 summer window. GCC's scope is the low-voltage package — structured cabling, IP video distribution, and access-control rough-in — per bid documents dated 2026-03-12.

OUR APPROACH

Scope of work.

Base bid covers all labor, materials, mobilization, Fluke DSX-8000 certification, and LinkWare PDF closeout for the scope lines below. Cat6A plenum is the GCC baseline cable, supplied at the spec's Cat6 price. Work is scheduled for the 10-week summer window; all substantive installation and testing complete before 2026-08-17 student return.

Warranty.

GCC LLC warrants all cabling workmanship for the lifetime of the installed plant at this property. Hardware we supply carries the manufacturer's warranty (five years standard unless noted otherwise) and we administer claims at no charge to the client for the first twelve months. The workmanship warranty transfers with the property if ownership changes; the client pays nothing to invoke it and we respond on the next business day to any written warranty notice. Warranty does not cover damage caused by fire, flood, rodent, third-party tampering, or modifications performed by others after our closeout.

What this quote covers.

Rachel, thanks for reaching out. This quote covers the AdHoc Center East Wing CCTV Expansion scope we discussed. Pricing is held firm through the date on the cover, and every cable we install carries our standard lifetime workmanship warranty.

AdHoc is expanding IP video coverage into the east wing following the 2024 buildout. Scope is cable + rough-in + camera mount; camera heads supplied by AdHoc's IT team and installed by GCC. Off-hours cutover preferred to minimize impact on programming.

We're the same crew that did your 2024 east-wing buildout, so we already know your IDF layout, your Genetec zone-mapping conventions, and your preferred cable routing through the ceiling grid. That keeps this job clean and fast.

What we'll do.

Each new camera position gets a Cat6A plenum home-run from the camera location back to the east-wing IDF, terminated at both ends. We'll mount, align, and commission each camera (heads by you). We'll integrate every new device into the existing Genetec VMS with the same zone-mapping conventions we set up in 2024. And we'll Fluke-certify every cable run, with the LinkWare PDF dropped in your SharePoint at closeout.

What lifetime warranty means for you.

If we put the cable in, we stand behind the cable — for as long as you own the property. The warranty transfers if you sell. You do not pay us to honor it.

GCC LLC warrants all cabling workmanship for the lifetime of the installed plant at this property. Hardware we supply carries the manufacturer's warranty (five years standard unless noted otherwise) and we administer claims at no charge to the client for the first twelve months. The workmanship warranty transfers with the property if ownership changes; the client pays nothing to invoke it and we respond on the next business day to any written warranty notice. Warranty does not cover damage caused by fire, flood, rodent, third-party tampering, or modifications performed by others after our closeout.

What's not in this quote.

Camera heads. Owner-furnished per the existing Axis M-series standard; we mount and commission.
VMS license expansion. Any Genetec licenses beyond device adds are billed by the publisher at AdHoc's expense.
Structural mount modification. Anything beyond the pre-approved mounting locations needs a change order.
Standard exclusions. Fire alarm, ERCES/BDA, lighting control, BMS/HVAC, mass notification, and line-voltage work.

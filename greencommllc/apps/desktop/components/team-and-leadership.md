## Crew & field standards (policy statement — always included)

- **Two-person minimum on site at all times.** GCC never dispatches solo techs, regardless of task size.
- **Default crew:** one foreman plus one laborer. Foreman-to-laborer ratio never exceeds 1:3.
- **BICSI-certified installers** available on request.
- Every foreman is trained on OSHA 10 minimum; OSHA 30 for supervisors.
- ICRA Class 3 and Class 4 training for healthcare and senior-living work.
